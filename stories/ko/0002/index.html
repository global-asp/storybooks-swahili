Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

Michelle Kim

Scarlet Kim

Korean

Level 1

이 동물들을 보세요.

Tazama wanyama.

소는 “음메” 라고 해요.

Ng’ombe anasema, “Moo.”

염소는 “메헤헤헤, 메헤헤.”

Mbuzi anasema, “Mee, mee.”

말은 “히히히힝.”

Farasi anasema, “Nei.”

돼지는 “꿀꿀.”

Nguruwe anasema, “Ngruu.”

닭은 “꼬꼬.”

Kuku anasema, “Ko, ko, ko.”

개는 “멍멍.”

Mbwa anasema, “Wouh, wouh.”

농부는 “쉿!” 이라 말해요.

Mkulima anasema, “Nyamaza!”

Written by: Jenny Katz
Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Michelle Kim
Read by: Scarlet Kim
Language: Korean
Level: Level 1
Source: Look at the animals from African Storybook
