Se på dyrene.

Tazama wanyama.

Koen siger “muh”.

Ng’ombe anasema, “Moo.”

Geden siger “bæh, bæh”.

Mbuzi anasema, “Mee, mee.”

Hesten siger “pruh”.

Farasi anasema, “Nei.”

Grisen siger “øf”.

Nguruwe anasema, “Ngruu.”

Hønen siger “gok”.

Kuku anasema, “Ko, ko, ko.”

Hunden siger “vov”.

Mbwa anasema, “Wouh, wouh.”

Landmanden siger “ssh!”

Mkulima anasema, “Nyamaza!”

Written by: Jenny Katz
Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Kim Sandvad West
Language: Danish
Level: Level 1
