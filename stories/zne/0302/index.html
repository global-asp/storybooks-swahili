Wee Moto

Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano

Rob Owen

Isaac Monday Elisama

Zande

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Mo ngere, wee!

Angalia, moto!

We na zohe azo.

Moto unachoma.

We na manga riahe.

Moto unatumika kupika.

We na fayasa kpoto boro ni wenehe.

Moto unaleta joto.

We na fu ima rago.

Moto unaleta mwanga.

Mo ngere, wee!

Angalia, moto!

We ni bawene he gbe.

Moto ni wa ajabu.

Na tina ome gbe.

Na una nguvu sana.

Written by: Deborah Namugosa, Beatrice Nabune, Alison Tukei, Rose Sabano
Illustrated by: Rob Owen
Translated by: Isaac Monday Elisama
Language: Zande
Level: Level 1
Source: Fire from African Storybook
